Training Offers

Step 1

Introductorary session to the basics of SPC that will provide you with all you need to know to make better decisions when using your data.

Step 2

Step by step demonstration of our easy to use Excel SPC tools to enable you to create your own charts and dashboards.

Step 3

Focussing on improving your narrative writing skills to describe the key messages from the data and prompt the correct action.

Step 4

Taking your SPC knowledge to a deeper level and providing you with tips on how to convert colleagues to use and understand SPC too!

Step 5

As well as looking at data over time, we help you to start thinking about comparing your data with others and the benefits of benchmarking.

Step 6

Once special cause is indetified, we present some improvement techniques and methodologies to help you respond to it.

Step 7

Introducing the concept of traiangulation which looking different sources and types of data together to provide a richer picture.
